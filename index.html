About

Climate Resilience and Environmental Sustainability in the Asian Water Tower (CREST) is a pioneering initiative led by Michigan State University to develop a Digital Twin (DT) of the Asian Water Tower (AWT)—a region critical to the water, energy, and food security of over 25% of the world’s population.

CREST aims to revolutionize convergence research by integrating high-resolution Earth system models, real-time data, AI/ML, and socioeconomic insights to understand and manage the complex interactions between climate, water systems, agriculture, and human activities. Through this cutting-edge DT platform, CREST will enable dynamic scenario testing, early warning systems, and decision-support tools that enhance climate resilience, promote sustainable development, and support equitable resource management across Asia and beyond.
